Before reproducing or investigating, do an intake:

1. **Restate the bug** — In 1-3 sentences: what's the reported behavior,
   what's expected, what triggers it.
2. **List your assumptions** — Up to 3: which component owns the behavior,
   which user flow exposes it, what counts as "fixed."
3. **Escalate if the restatement is hollow** — If the symptom, the trigger,
   or the expected behavior is unclear from the description, stop and use
   `mcp__cydo__Ask` before spawning a reproducer. Investigating the wrong
   symptom is the most common way bug tasks waste their context.
